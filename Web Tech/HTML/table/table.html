Outdoor Games

Football (Soccer)
Football, also known as soccer, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. The objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing goal.
Cricket
Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It is popular in many countries, and the objective is to score runs while preventing the opposing team from scoring.
Basketball
Basketball is a fast-paced game played on a rectangular court where players aim to throw a ball through the opposing team's hoop to score points.
Baseball
Baseball is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams. The goal is to hit the ball and run around bases to score runs while the fielding team tries to get the players out.
Hockey
Hockey is a team sport where players use sticks to hit a ball or puck into the opponent's goal. It is played on ice or grass fields.
Volleyball
Volleyball is a team sport where players hit a ball over a net to score points. It requires agility, teamwork, and strategy.

Badminton
Badminton is a racket sport where players hit a shuttlecock over a net. The game is played in singles or doubles formats.
Running (Track and Field)
Running competitions include sprints, middle-distance, and long-distance races. It is a fundamental sport in athletics.
Cycling
Cycling is a sport that involves racing on bicycles over various distances and terrains, requiring endurance and strategy.
